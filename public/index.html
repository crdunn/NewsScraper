The Onion, or NotTheOnion?

Sometimes the truth is just as surreal as absurdist comedy!

Founded in 1988, The Onion is a satirical newspaper that publishes parody news articles. Topics covered range from satire of current political events, to surreal absurdist pieces, to dramatic reporting on mundane events. The company has successfully created a unique and recognizable style to their satire, and its audience can clearly recognize “an Onion article” from the headline alone.

However, sometimes the truth can be just as strange as a comedian’s imagination. The subreddit /r/nottheonion is an online community dedicated to cataloging real news reports that sound like they were written by The Onion.
